NZ Farmer Counselor AI

Project Tūrangawaewae

For those who carry the land on their shoulders

Built with the rural heart in mind, this AI assistant supports New Zealand farmers through stress, burnout, and emotional hardship. It listens without judgment, speaks without fluff, and stands quietly beside those who walk the long paddocks alone.

"You’re not weak. You’re weathered. Let’s face the next season together."

This project combines AI technology with 9+ years of grassroots insights from Aotearoa’s farming community. Designed to be practical, culturally aware, and emotionally grounded — not a replacement for human contact, but a steady companion in between.
